Intern Quick Start
Marquee GTM Operator

Your AI go-to-market teammate for the Marquee launch

It drafts content, outreach, surveys, and research in Sarah's voice — and never sends anything to the public without a yes. Here's everything you need on one page.

1 Start it up

Open the Terminal app, then type these two lines:

cd ~/Desktop/ROO/marquee claude

First time only: a browser may ask you to log in with the Anthropic account. To quit: type /exit.

2 Talk to the operator

Begin every message with the words below, then ask for what you want in plain English:

Use the marquee-gtm-operator …

! The golden rule

The operator will draft, research, and organize freely. It will never post, email, DM, or publish anything in Marquee's name without showing you first. Anything going public is always your call. Work with it freely — nothing goes live behind your back.

3 Feed its memory

Just tell it — e.g. "Use the marquee-gtm-operator. A user said '…' — log it in feedback." Or type into the files yourself (always add to the bottom, keep the date):

  • feedbackwhat real users say — paste word-for-word
  • competitorswho else is out there + their gaps
  • marketstats & survey data — every number needs a source
  • decisionswhat we decided & why

Files live in .claude/skills/marquee-gtm/knowledge/

★ Prompts you'll actually use

Where do we stand?
Use the marquee-gtm-operator. Figure out which stage the launch is on and tell me the single most important next move.
Test the Blueprint
Use the marquee-gtm-operator. Draft short outreach to 10–15 people who are job-hunting or pivoting, asking them to try the Alignment Blueprint. Sarah's voice. Don't send — show me first.
First survey (own the data)
Use the marquee-gtm-operator. Draft our first survey. Pick the topic with the best PR potential, write the questions, and save the plan to the knowledge base.
Log feedback
Use the marquee-gtm-operator. A beta user said: "[paste]". Save it to the feedback file word-for-word, note the theme, and tell me if it changes anything.
Scout competitors
Use the marquee-gtm-operator. Research who competes with Marquee, rank them, note their weak spots, and save it to the competitors file.

You don't have to memorize these. Describe what you want in normal English — the operator handles the rest.
