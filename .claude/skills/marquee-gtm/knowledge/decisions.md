### 2026-06-19 — Foundational decisions (captured from the strategy spine at setup)

These are the load-bearing calls already baked into the model. They are settled unless a dated entry below reverses them.

- **B2C, consumer/creator-led motion. Not B2B, not sales-led.** Why: Marquee is a $46/yr product individuals buy themselves; an outbound/pipeline motion wastes months here.
- **v1 is candidate-side only.** No company login, search, discovery, matching, or hiring tools. Those are v2. Why: keep the surface area and the story focused on the person in motion.
- **Pricing: $46/yr**, framed as "one coffee a month." A lever to test, not a permanent setting.
- **No LinkedIn yet. Paid ads parked.** Channel order is Instagram first, then TikTok, Reddit seeded, SEO/GEO compounding, email owned.
- **The brand is the marketing; trust first, product last.** Build audience by creating/curating/connecting, then launch into it. Do not lead with the product.
- **Self-serve only.** No demos, no sales calls, no "book a demo."
- **Build with creators, never cold-barter them.** A handful of real relationships, not fifty cold DMs.
- **No invented growth mechanics or gimmicks.** If a tactic sounds like a growth hack, it is cut.
- **WFH (the media property) is deprioritized.** Do not make it central or assume it runs.
