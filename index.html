I · Impact — Career Highlights
3× Pipeline Growth at Lattice
Built the growth function from zero — hired the team, chose the stack, designed the playbook. Inbound pipeline grew from $4M to $13M ARR contribution in 18 months. Core lever: shifted budget from brand to high-intent SEO and bottom-of-funnel retargeting.
Rebuilt Figma Activation → 34% to 61% Retention
Identified that 60% of churned users never completed a second project. Redesigned the Day 1 experience around template completion instead of blank canvas. 30-day retention improved from 34% to 61% in 90 days. This one change compounded across every acquisition channel.
Notion Referral Program — 40% of Early Growth
Designed and shipped Notion's first referral program as employee 23. Drove 40% of user acquisition in the first year post-launch. The mechanic: give credit, not cash — turned sharing into a status signal for early adopters.
I · Insights
What most people in my field get wrong
Most growth teams chase channels instead of conversion. The real leverage is almost always in the middle of the funnel — not the top. I've seen companies 10× their CAC efficiency just by fixing what happens after the click.
What I changed my mind about
I used to think data was the answer to every growth question. Now I know data tells you what happened — it almost never tells you why. The best insights I've had came from a 20-minute user interview, not a dashboard.
What I bring that doesn't show up in a JD
I can read a dataset and tell you what question it's actually answering — then reframe the whole strategy around the right one. Most teams are solving the wrong problem with great execution.
